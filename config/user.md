# User Profile: Riley Parker

## How I work
I open my browser in the morning and want a brief — what changed since
last night, what's on today, what needs me. I don't want to dig.

## Where I am
Brooklyn, NY. America/New_York.

## Preferences
- Visual: timelines and lists over dense tables.
- Tone: direct. No "great question" or "I noticed you might want to…".
- Surface what's *new* or *needs attention* — skip the routine.

## Standing concerns
- Inbox health — flagged email and recent unread
- Calendar today — what's coming up and what needs prep
- Messages across chat apps — DMs only, not channel chatter
- News from a small set of feeds I actually follow
- Weather — current and what to expect later today
- Recently edited docs — what I was last working on
